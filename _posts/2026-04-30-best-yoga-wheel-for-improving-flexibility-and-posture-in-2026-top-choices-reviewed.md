## Why Use a Yoga Wheel?

### Enhancing Flexibility

For anyone serious about yoga, increasing flexibility isn't just an option; it's a prerequisite for advancing in your practice. A yoga wheel helps in stretching areas that often go neglected.

- **Hip Openers**: The wheel's arc provides stability when you achieve certain poses. This assists in deepening stretches that focus on hip mobility, essential for many yoga practices.
- **Backbends**: Using a yoga wheel can help release tension in the spine and shoulders, enabling deeper backbends without a fear of injury.

### Improving Posture

Rounding your spine or sitting with improper alignment can lead to various issues. A yoga wheel aids in establishing better posture.

- **Spinal Alignment**: You can roll your spine over the wheel to relieve pressure points and tension, enhancing spinal alignment.
- **Core Strengthening**: Balancing on the wheel also activates core muscles, promoting better overall posture.
