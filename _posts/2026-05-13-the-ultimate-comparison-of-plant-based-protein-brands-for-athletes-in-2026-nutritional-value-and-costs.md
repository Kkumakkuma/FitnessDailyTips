## How Does Plant-Based Protein Work?

Plant-based protein powders work by providing your body with essential amino acids often sourced from legumes, grains, seeds, and nuts. These proteins serve as the building blocks for muscle repair and growth. When consumed, they help repair muscle tissue and promote recovery after intense workouts—making them a vital addition to any athlete's diet.

Most plant-based protein powders offer a balanced amino acid profile. However, they may lack certain amino acids like lysine, which is abundant in animal proteins. This makes understanding the composition of your chosen brand essential.
