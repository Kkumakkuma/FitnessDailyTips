## Understanding Protein and Muscle Gain

### Why Protein Matters for Muscle Growth

Protein is vital for muscle repair and growth. When you work out, you create tiny tears in your muscle fibers. Protein helps repair these tears, making your muscles stronger and bigger. 

- **Muscle Recovery**: Studies indicate that post-workout protein intake can increase muscle synthesis by 15%-25%.
- **Daily Recommendations**: For muscle gain, bodybuilders often consume around 1.6-2.2 grams of protein per kilogram of body weight per day.

### Types of Protein Powders

Understanding the types of protein can be overwhelming. Here's a quick overview:

- **Whey Protein**: Fast-absorbing and ideal for post-workout recovery. Great for muscle gain.
- **Casein Protein**: Slower absorption, making it perfect before bedtime to aid muscle recovery overnight.
- **Plant-Based Proteins**: Options like pea protein and brown rice protein are excellent for vegans or those lactose intolerant.

With these basics in mind, let’s dive into the best protein powders for muscle gain in 2026!
