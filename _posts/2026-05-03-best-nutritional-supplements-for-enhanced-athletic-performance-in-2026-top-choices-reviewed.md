## What Most Guides Get Wrong

1. **Creatine is Only for Bulking**: Many guides still promote creatine solely for muscle gain.  
   **Why this matters:** Creatine also enhances performance in endurance activities by replenishing ATP, as I discovered when I upped my running mileage earlier this year.

2. **Plant-Based Proteins Aren't as Effective**: There's a myth that plant-based proteins don't compare to whey.  
   **Why this matters:** I found that pea protein can deliver similar muscle gains when combined with other plant proteins, which helped me transition to a more vegetarian routine without sacrificing results.

3. **BCAAs Aren't Necessary with Protein**: Some experts argue BCAAs are redundant if you're already consuming sufficient protein.  
   **Why this matters:** I experienced greatly reduced muscle soreness when taking BCAAs right after my workouts, showing they can still play a crucial role even with adequate protein intake.
