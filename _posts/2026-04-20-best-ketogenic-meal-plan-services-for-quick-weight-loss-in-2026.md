## What is a Ketogenic Meal Plan?

The ketogenic diet is a low-carb, high-fat eating plan designed to put your body into a state of ketosis. When in ketosis, your body flips from burning carbohydrates for energy to burning fat. This process can accelerate weight loss, but it can be tricky to manage on your own. Ideally, you need a well-structured meal plan to hit the right macro ratios.

### How Ketogenic Meal Plans Work

A typical ketogenic meal plan consists of:

- **High Fat**: About 70-75% of your daily caloric intake.
- **Moderate Protein**: Approximately 20-25%.
- **Low Carbohydrates**: Usually around 5-10%.

By focusing on these macronutrient guidelines, many individuals find they can lose weight effectively, as I did.
